HOW TO PLAY!

  • Use Left and Right Arrow Keys to Move
  • Hold Down Space Bar to Shoot
  • 10 Points for Space Junk
  • 500 Points for the Slow-Moving Gems
  • Space Junk Increase in Speed, and Spawn Faster Each Level
  • Getting Hit by Space Junk or a Gem Will Stop the Game
  • Staying still and holding down space bar won't get you very far!
  • Good Luck, and Try to Beat Your High Score!
